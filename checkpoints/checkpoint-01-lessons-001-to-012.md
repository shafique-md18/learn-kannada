# Checkpoint 01: Lessons 001-012

Use this with a practice assistant or for self-practice after finishing Lesson 012.

Do not check lesson files while answering.

## Part 1: Pattern Recall

Say these in Kannada:

1. This is water.
2. That is office.
3. What is this?
4. I want tea.
5. I do not want coffee.
6. This is not shop.
7. Where is the lift?
8. Office is there.
9. Inside the room.
10. Outside the gate.
11. Who are you?
12. Who is he/she?

## Part 2: Action Recall

Say these in Kannada:

1. Come here.
2. Go there.
3. Look at this.
4. Give the bill.
5. Take this.
6. Wait a little.
7. Sit here.
8. Stop here.
9. Tell the address.
10. Show the room.
11. Go straight.
12. Stop here.

## Part 3: Mixed Situations

Respond in Kannada.

1. You enter an office building. Ask where the lift is.
2. The guard points far away. Say "there?"
3. You see a closed room. Ask what it is.
4. Someone offers coffee, but you want tea.
5. A delivery agent calls. Tell him to come outside the gate.
6. The cab driver asks if you are coming. Say yes, you are coming now.
7. Ask maintenance if they will come now.
8. Security tells you to wait. Say okay.

## Part 4: Roleplay Prompts

### Roleplay A: Apartment Gate

You are at your apartment gate. Ask the security guard:

1. Who is that person?
2. Where is the room?
3. Please show the room.
4. Okay, I will come now.

### Roleplay B: Delivery Call

The delivery agent cannot find you.

Say:

1. Come here.
2. I am outside the gate.
3. Wait a little.
4. I am coming now.

### Roleplay C: Auto Direction Basics

You are in an auto.

Say:

1. Go straight.
2. Take left turn.
3. Stop here.
4. Take this bill.

## Part 5: Listening Prediction

If someone says this, what do they probably mean?

1. Illi banni.
2. Swalpa kaayiri.
3. Lift alli.
4. Ivaga agalla.
5. Naan barthini.
6. Idu office alla.
7. Address heli.
8. Right hogi.

## Part 6: Build Your Own

Create 10 original Kannada sentences using only patterns from Lessons 001-012.

Rules:

- At least 3 questions.
- At least 3 action/request sentences.
- At least 2 location sentences.
- At least 1 sentence with `beku` or `beda`.
- At least 1 sentence with `naan`.
