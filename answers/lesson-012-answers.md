# Lesson 012 Answers

## Guided Practice Answers

1. Naan barthini.
2. Naan hogthini.
3. Naan madthini.
4. Ivaga barthini.

## Production Practice Sample Answers

1. Ivaga barthini.
2. Alli hogthini.
3. Idu aamele madthini.
4. Innu water beku.

## Roleplay Sample

Haudu, ivaga barthini. Swalpa kaayiri.

## Quiz Answers

1. `barthini` = I am coming / I will come.
2. `hogthini` = I am going / I will go.
3. I will do it.
4. Innu coffee beku.

## Natural Bangalore Versions

- Ivaga barthini is natural when a delivery agent or guard is waiting.
- Swalpa kaayiri pairs well with it.

## Common Mistakes

- `barthira` = will you come? `barthini` = I will come.
