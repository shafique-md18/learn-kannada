# Lesson 007 Answers

## Guided Practice Answers

1. Illi banni.
2. Alli hogi.
3. Idu nodi.
4. Bill kodi.

## Production Practice Sample Answers

1. Illi banni.
2. Water kodi.
3. Idu nodi.
4. Idu bill tagoli.

## Roleplay Sample

Illi banni. Naan gate horage.

## Quiz Answers

1. `banni` = come respectfully.
2. `hogi` = go respectfully.
3. Look at this.
4. Coffee kodi.

## Natural Bangalore Versions

- Illi banni is very common and polite.
- Bill kodi is the natural "give the bill."

## Common Mistakes

- `kodi` = give; `tagoli` = take. Do not swap them.
