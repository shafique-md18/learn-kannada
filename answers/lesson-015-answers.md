# Lesson 015 Answers

## Guided Practice Answers

1. Innu water beku.
2. Saaku.
3. Swalpa kadime madi.
4. Chikka packet beku.

## Production Practice Sample Answers

1. Innu tea beku.
2. Saaku.
3. Dodda packet beku.
4. Hechchu beda.

## Roleplay Sample

Swalpa kadime madi. Saaku.

## Quiz Answers

1. `saaku` = enough.
2. `kadime` = less.
3. I do not want more / too much not wanted.
4. Innu coffee beku.

## Natural Bangalore Versions

- Saaku is a powerful one-word daily response.
- Swalpa kadime madi is polite and practical.

## Common Mistakes

- `innu` means more/still. `saaku` means enough.
