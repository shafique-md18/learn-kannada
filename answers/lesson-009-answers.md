# Lesson 009 Answers

## Guided Practice Answers

1. Number heli.
2. Name heli.
3. Room torisi.
4. Idu torisi.

## Production Practice Sample Answers

1. Lift torisi.
2. Address heli.

4. Gothilla.

## Roleplay Sample

Room elli?

Room torisi.

## Quiz Answers

1. `heli` = tell/say.
2. `torisi` = show.
3. Tell the number.
4. Show this.

## Natural Bangalore Versions

- Address heli is natural over the phone.
- Lift torisi is practical with security or building staff.

## Common Mistakes

- Use `heli` for information and `torisi` for showing physically or visually.
