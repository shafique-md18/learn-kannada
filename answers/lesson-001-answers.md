## Quiz Answers

1. `idu` = this.
2. `adu` = that.
3. Idu enu?
4. Adu mane.

## Natural Bangalore Versions

- Idu enu? is natural and common.
- Idu water is acceptable in daily mixed speech.

## Common Mistakes

- Do not add an English "is" inside Kannada: avoid `Idu is water`.
