# Lesson 013 Answers

## Guided Practice Answers

1. Idannu kodi.
2. Adannu kodi.
3. Ondu tea kodi.
4. Eradu packet kodi.

## Production Practice Sample Answers

1. Idannu kodi.
2. Ondu coffee kodi.
3. Eradu tea kodi.
4. Idannu tagoli.

## Roleplay Sample

Ondu packet kodi.

## Quiz Answers

1. `idu` = this is; `idannu` = this object.
2. `ondu` = one.
3. `eradu` = two.
4. Adannu kodi.

## Natural Bangalore Versions

- Idannu kodi is natural when pointing at something.
- Ondu tea kodi is natural at a stall.

## Common Mistakes

- Use `idannu` when "this" receives the action: give this, take this, see this.
