2. Repeat madi.
3. Clear aagi heli.
4. Kelistilla.

## Roleplay Sample

## Quiz Answers

1. Please say one more time.
2. Repeat madi.
3. Innondu sala heli.
4. Sari.

## Natural Bangalore Versions

- `Innondu sala heli.` is a practical Bangalore sentence; the English loan words are acceptable where locals use them.

## Common Mistakes

- Do not force a long sentence. Build two short sentences if needed.
