# Lesson 010 Answers

## Guided Practice Answers

1. Left hogi.
2. Right hogi.
3. Straight hogi.
4. Illi stop madi.

## Production Practice Sample Answers

1. Straight hogi, aamele right hogi.
2. Illi stop madi.
3. Left turn madi.
4. Alli hogi, swalpa kaayiri.

## Roleplay Sample

Straight hogi. Left turn madi. Illi stop madi.

## Quiz Answers

1. `madi` means do, used politely after many action words.
2. Go right.
3. Stop here.
4. Straight hogi.

## Natural Bangalore Versions

- Straight hogi and left turn madi are natural Bangalore directions.
- Stop madi is common in auto/cab contexts.

## Common Mistakes

- English direction words are okay here because locals commonly use them.
