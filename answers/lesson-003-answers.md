# Lesson 003 Answers

## Guided Practice Answers

1. Idu shop alla.
2. Idu water alla.
3. Haudu, tea beku.
4. Illa, coffee beda.

## Production Practice Sample Answers

1. Idu shop alla. Idu office.
2. Coffee beda.
3. Sari.
4. Paravagilla.

## Roleplay Sample

Illa, idu office alla. Idu shop.

## Quiz Answers

1. `haudu` = yes.
2. `illa` = no/not there.
3. `alla` makes a simple "is not" sentence.
4. Idu mane alla.

## Natural Bangalore Versions

- Illa, idu office alla is a clear natural correction.
- Paravagilla is useful when someone says sorry or there is a small issue.

## Common Mistakes

- `illa` is a general no/not there. `alla` is used for "is not" identity sentences.
