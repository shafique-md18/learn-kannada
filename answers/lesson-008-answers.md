# Lesson 008 Answers

## Guided Practice Answers

1. Swalpa kaayiri.
2. Illi koothkolli.
3. Illi nilli.
4. Swalpa nodi.

## Production Practice Sample Answers

1. Swalpa kaayiri.
2. Illi koothkolli.
3. Illi nilli.
4. Swalpa idu nodi.

## Roleplay Sample

Sari. Barthini.

## Quiz Answers

1. `kaayiri` = wait.
2. `koothkolli` = sit.
3. `swalpa` softens requests and means little/some.
4. Stop/stand here.

## Natural Bangalore Versions

- Swalpa kaayiri is more polite than only Kaayiri.
- Illi nilli works for "stop here" in a practical context.

## Common Mistakes

- `swalpa` does not always literally mean a small quantity. In requests, it often makes the sentence softer.
