# Lesson 002 Answers

## Guided Practice Answers

1. Tea beku.
2. Coffee beda.
3. Bill beku.
4. Swalpa water beku.

## Production Practice Sample Answers

1. Coffee beku.
2. Tea beda.
3. Bill beku.
4. Swalpa water beku.

## Roleplay Sample

Tea beda. Coffee beku.

## Quiz Answers

1. `beku` = want/need.
2. `beda` = do not want.
3. I do not want coffee.
4. Swalpa water beku.

## Natural Bangalore Versions

- Coffee beku is natural at a shop or stall.
- Swalpa water beku sounds softer than only Water beku.

## Common Mistakes

- Put the item before `beku` or `beda`: `Tea beku`, not `beku tea`.
