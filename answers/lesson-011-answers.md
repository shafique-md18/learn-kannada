# Lesson 011 Answers

## Guided Practice Answers

1. Neevu barthira?
2. Alli hogthira?
3. Idu madthira?
4. Ivaga barthira?

## Production Practice Sample Answers

1. Neevu barthira?
2. Ivaga barthira?
3. Alli hogthira?
4. Bill kodthira?

## Roleplay Sample

Ivaga barthira? Agalla andre, later barthira?

## Quiz Answers

1. `barthira` = will you come?
2. `hogthira` = will you go?
3. Will you come now?
4. `agalla` = cannot / will not work.

## Natural Bangalore Versions

- Ivaga barthira? is useful for maintenance and delivery calls.
- Agalla is a common practical reply meaning it will not happen/work.

## Common Mistakes

- `barthira` asks the other person. `barthini` means I am coming.
