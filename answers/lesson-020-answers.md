# Lesson 020 Answers

## Guided Practice Answers

1. Meter alli barthira?
2. Yeshtu agutte?
3. Extra beda.
4. Fixed rate beda.

## Production Practice Sample Answers

1. Meter alli barthira?
2. Yeshtu agutte?
3. Extra beda.
4. Metro hogbeku.

## Roleplay Sample

Meter alli barthira? Office hogbeku. Extra beda.

## Quiz Answers

1. `meter alli` = by meter/on meter.
2. How much will it be?
3. Extra beda.
4. Ivaga barthira?

## Natural Bangalore Versions

- Meter alli barthira? is natural but may sound firm. Add `sari na?` later if softening.

## Common Mistakes

- `eshtu` = how much now/general; `yeshtu agutte` = how much will it become/be.
