# Lesson 004 Answers

## Guided Practice Answers

1. Office elli?
2. Room elli?
3. Lift alli.
4. Water illi.

## Production Practice Sample Answers

1. Shop elli?
2. Washroom elli?
3. Bill elli?
4. Mane alli.

## Roleplay Sample

You: Lift elli?

Guard: Alli.

You: Sari.

## Quiz Answers

1. `elli` = where.
2. `illi` = here.
3. `alli` = there.
4. Where is the shop?

## Natural Bangalore Versions

- Lift elli? and Washroom elli? are very natural building-level questions.

## Common Mistakes

- Keep `elli` after the place/object: `Lift elli?`, not `Elli lift?`.
