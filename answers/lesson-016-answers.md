# Lesson 016 Answers

## Guided Practice Answers

1. Tea ideya?
2. Fresh ideya?
3. Coffee illa.
4. Sigutta?

## Production Practice Sample Answers

1. Colgate ideya?
2. Change ideya?
3. Water ide.
4. Stock illa.

## Roleplay Sample

You: Medicine ideya?

Shopkeeper: Ivaga sigalla.

## Quiz Answers

1. `ide` = is there/exists.
2. `ideya` = is there?
3. `sigutta` = will it be available?
4. Coffee is not there.

## Natural Bangalore Versions

- ___ ideya? is one of the most useful shop patterns.
- Ivaga sigalla is a natural shopkeeper-style reply.

## Common Mistakes

- `ide` is a statement. `ideya?` is a question.
