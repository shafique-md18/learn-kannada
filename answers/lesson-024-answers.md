# Lesson 024 Answers

## Guided Practice Answers

1. Reach aadmele call madi.
2. Wait madi.
3. Confirm madi.
4. Meter okay na?

## Production Practice Sample Answers

1. Reach aadmele call madi.
2. Gate horage wait madi.

Meter alli barthira?

## Quiz Answers

1. `aadmele` = after.
2. Call.
3. Call after reaching.
4. Gate horage wait madi.

## Natural Bangalore Versions

- Reach aadmele call madi is common mixed Bangalore speech.
- Gate horage wait madi is clear for drivers.

## Common Mistakes

- `aadmele` goes after the first action/event: Reach aadmele.
