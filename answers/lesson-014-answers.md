# Lesson 014 Answers

## Guided Practice Answers

1. Bill eshtu?
2. Idannu eshtu?
3. Change ideya?
4. Water ideya?

## Production Practice Sample Answers

1. Rate eshtu?
2. Change ideya?
3. Rate jaasti.
4. Packet eshtu?

## Roleplay Sample

Idannu eshtu? Change ideya?

## Quiz Answers

1. `eshtu` = how much/how many.
2. `ideya` = is there?
3. How much is the bill?
4. Water ideya?

## Natural Bangalore Versions

- Rate eshtu? is common and practical.
- Idannu eshtu? is useful while pointing to an item.

## Common Mistakes

- `eshtu` asks amount/number. `elli` asks location.
