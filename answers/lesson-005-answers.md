# Lesson 005 Answers

## Guided Practice Answers

1. Bengaluru alli.
2. Office alli.
3. Room olage.
4. Gate horage.

## Production Practice Sample Answers

1. Shop horage.
2. Water room olage.
3. Mane Bengaluru alli.
4. Lift apartment alli.

## Roleplay Sample

Apartment alli. Gate horage banni.

## Quiz Answers

1. In place phrases, `alli` can mean in/at.
2. `olage` = inside.
3. `horage` = outside.
4. In Bengaluru.

## Natural Bangalore Versions

- Apartment alli is natural for "in/at the apartment."
- Gate horage banni is a practical way to say "come outside the gate."

## Common Mistakes

- `alli` can mean "there" or "in/at"; context tells the meaning.
