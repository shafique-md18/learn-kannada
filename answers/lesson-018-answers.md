## Quiz Answers

1. `togothini` = I will take.
2. That is all / all enough.
3. This is okay, right?
4. Ondu packet togothini.

## Natural Bangalore Versions

- Idannu togothini is natural after asking price.
- Ella saaku is useful when ending a shop order.

## Common Mistakes

- `tagoli` = you take. `togothini` = I will take.
