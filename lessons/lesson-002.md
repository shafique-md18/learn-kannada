## Main Pattern

### Kannada Sentence

Water beku.

### Hindi Pronunciation

वॉटर बेकु.

### Meaning

Hindi: पानी चाहिए.

English: I want water / Water is needed.

## Word-by-Word Breakdown

| Word | Hindi Meaning | English Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| water | पानी | water |
| beku | चाहिए | want/need |

## Sentence Construction Logic

In Kannada, the thing you want comes first, then `beku`.

`Water beku` literally: water want/needed.

For "do not want", replace `beku` with `beda`.

## Reusable Pattern

`___ beku` = I want/need ___

Examples:

- Tea beku.
- Coffee beku.
- Bill beku.

`___ beda` = I do not want ___

Examples:

- Tea beda.
- Coffee beda.
- Bill beda.

## Guided Practice

Build these in Kannada:

1. I want tea.
2. I do not want coffee.
3. I want the bill.
4. I want some water.

## Production Practice

Say these in Kannada:

1. At a tea stall, ask for coffee.
2. At a restaurant, say you do not want tea.
3. At a shop, ask for the bill.
4. At home, ask for some water.

## Real-Life Bangalore Roleplay

Reply in Kannada using two short sentences.
