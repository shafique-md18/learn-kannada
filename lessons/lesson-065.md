## Main Pattern

### Kannada Sentence

Swalpa time beku.

### Hindi Pronunciation

स्वल्प टाइम बेकु.

### Meaning

Hindi: थोड़ा समय चाहिए.

English: Need some time.

## Word-by-Word Breakdown

| Word | Hindi Meaning | English Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| Swalpa | थोड़ा | little/please |
| time | समय चाहिए | need time |
| beku | चाहिए | want/need |

## Sentence Construction Logic

This lesson extends the reusable pattern `Staff reply chunks`. Keep the sentence short first, then add time/place/person words.

## Reusable Pattern

`Staff reply chunks`

Examples:

- Ivaga busy.
- Aamele barthini.
- Nodthini.
