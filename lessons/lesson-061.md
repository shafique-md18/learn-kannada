## Main Pattern

### Kannada Sentence

Gotha.

### Hindi Pronunciation

गोत्ता.

### Meaning

Hindi: पता है.

English: I know.

## Sentence Construction Logic

This lesson extends the reusable pattern `Short reply`. Keep the sentence short first, then add time/place/person words.
