## Main Pattern

### Kannada Sentence

Coffee ideya?

### Hindi Pronunciation

कॉफी इदेया?

### Meaning

Hindi: कॉफी है क्या?

English: Is coffee available?

## Word-by-Word Breakdown

| Word | Hindi Meaning | English Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| coffee | कॉफी | coffee |
| ideya | है क्या? | is there? |

## Sentence Construction Logic

Use `ideya?` to ask if something exists or is available.

Use `ide` to say it is there. Use `illa` to say it is not there.

## Reusable Pattern

`___ ideya?` = Is ___ there/available?

Examples:

- Coffee ideya?
- Fresh ideya?
- Change ideya?

Replies:

- Ide. = It is there.
- Illa. = It is not there.
- Sigutta? = Will it be available?
- Sigalla. = It will not be available.
