## Main Pattern

### Kannada Sentence

Neevu ellinda?

### Hindi Pronunciation

नीवु एल्लिन्दा?

### Meaning

Hindi: आप कहां से हैं?

English: Where are you from?

## Word-by-Word Breakdown

| Word | Hindi Meaning | English Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| Neevu | आप | you |
| ellinda | कहां से | from where |

## Sentence Construction Logic

This lesson extends the reusable pattern `Neevu ellinda?`. Keep the sentence short first, then add time/place/person words.

## Reusable Pattern

`Neevu ellinda?`

Examples:

- Neevu ellinda?
- Naan Delhi inda.
- Bangalore alli irthini.
- Eshtu varsha?

## Guided Practice

Build these in Kannada:

1. Where are you from?
2. I am from Delhi.
3. I live in Bangalore.
4. How many years?

## Production Practice

Say these in Kannada:

1. In a real Bangalore situation: Where are you from?
2. Change the place/person/item and say: I am from Delhi.
3. Use as the next line in a short conversation: I live in Bangalore.
4. Combine old knowledge with today: How many years?

## Real-Life Bangalore Roleplay

Tell a neighbour you are from Delhi and live in Bangalore.
