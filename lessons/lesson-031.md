## Main Pattern

### Kannada Sentence

Ivattu office hogbeku.

### Hindi Pronunciation

इवत्तु ऑफिस होग्बेकु.

### Meaning

Hindi: आज ऑफिस जाना है.

English: I need to go to office today.

## Word-by-Word Breakdown

| Word | Hindi Meaning | English Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| Ivattu | आज | today |
| office | ऑफिस | office |
| hogbeku | जाना है | need to go |

## Sentence Construction Logic

This lesson extends the reusable pattern `Time + sentence`. Keep the sentence short first, then add time/place/person words.

## Reusable Pattern

`Time + sentence`

Examples:

- Ivattu office hogbeku.
- Naale barthini.
- Ninne payment aayta?
- Sanje meet madi.

## Guided Practice

Build these in Kannada:

1. Today I need to go to office.
2. I will come tomorrow.
3. Payment happened yesterday?
4. Meet in evening.

## Production Practice

Say these in Kannada:

1. In a real Bangalore situation: Today I need to go to office.
2. Change the place/person/item and say: I will come tomorrow.
3. Use as the next line in a short conversation: Payment happened yesterday?
4. Combine old knowledge with today: Meet in evening.
