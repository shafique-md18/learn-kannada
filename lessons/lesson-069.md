## Main Pattern

### Kannada Sentence

Bathroom leak aagide.

### Hindi Pronunciation

बाथरूम लीक आगिदे.

### Meaning

Hindi: बाथरूम में लीकेज है.

English: Bathroom is leaking.

## Sentence Construction Logic

This lesson extends the reusable pattern `Repair sequence`. Keep the sentence short first, then add time/place/person words.

## Reusable Pattern

`Repair sequence`

Examples:

- Bathroom leak aagide.
- Pipe check madi.
- Plumber kalisi.
- Immediate barbeku.
