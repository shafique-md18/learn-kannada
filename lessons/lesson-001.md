## Main Pattern

### Kannada Sentence

Idu enu?

### Hindi Pronunciation

इदु एनु?

### Meaning

Hindi: यह क्या है?

English: What is this?

## Word-by-Word Breakdown

| Word | Hindi Meaning | English Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| idu | यह | this |
| enu | क्या | what |

## Sentence Construction Logic

Kannada often does not need a separate word for "is" in simple sentences.

Idu enu? literally means "This what?" but naturally means "What is this?"

## Reusable Pattern

`Idu ___` = This is ___

Examples:

- Idu water. = This is water.
- Idu office. = This is office.
- Idu mane. = This is home/house.

`Adu ___` = That is ___

Examples:

- Adu water. = That is water.
- Adu office. = That is office.
- Adu mane. = That is home/house.
