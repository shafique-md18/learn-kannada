## Main Pattern

### Kannada Sentence

Weekend en madthira?

### Hindi Pronunciation

वीकेंड एन मद्तिरा?

### Meaning

Hindi: वीकेंड पर क्या करते हैं?

English: What do you do on weekends?

## Word-by-Word Breakdown

| Word | Hindi Meaning | English Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| Weekend | वीकेंड | weekend |
| en | क्या | what |
| madthira | करते हैं? | do you do? |

## Sentence Construction Logic

This lesson extends the reusable pattern `Weekend ___ madthira?`. Keep the sentence short first, then add time/place/person words.

## Reusable Pattern

`Weekend ___ madthira?`

Examples:

- Weekend en madthira?
- Movie nodthini.
- Walk hogthini.
- Rest madthini.

## Guided Practice

Build these in Kannada:

1. What do you do on weekend?
2. I watch movie.
3. I go for walk.
4. I rest.

## Production Practice

Say these in Kannada:

1. In a real Bangalore situation: What do you do on weekend?
2. Change the place/person/item and say: I watch movie.
3. Use as the next line in a short conversation: I go for walk.
4. Combine old knowledge with today: I rest.
