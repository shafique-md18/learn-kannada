## Main Pattern

### Kannada Sentence

UPI ideya?

### Hindi Pronunciation

यूपीआई इदेया?

### Meaning

Hindi: यूपीआई है क्या?

English: Is UPI available?

## Word-by-Word Breakdown

| Word | Hindi Meaning | English Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| UPI | यूपीआई | UPI |
| ideya | है क्या? | is there? |

## Sentence Construction Logic

Use daily English payment words naturally with Kannada patterns.

`Payment madthini` = I will do payment.

## Reusable Pattern

`___ madthini` = I will do ___

Examples:

- Payment madthini.
- Scan madthini.

Requests:

- Bag beku.
- Receipt kodi.
- Cash illa.
