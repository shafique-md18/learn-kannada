## Main Pattern

### Kannada Sentence

Idu check madi.

### Hindi Pronunciation

इदु चेक माडि.

### Meaning

Hindi: यह चेक कीजिए.

English: Please check this.

## Word-by-Word Breakdown

| Word | Hindi Meaning | English Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| idu | यह | this |
| check madi | चेक कीजिए | check |

## Sentence Construction Logic

For staff requests, combine the object with `check/fix/repair madi`.

## Reusable Pattern

`Object + check/fix/repair madi`

Examples:

- Idu check madi.
- Fan fix madi.
- Door repair madi.
- Ivaga barthira?

## Guided Practice
