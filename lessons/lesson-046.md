## Main Pattern

### Kannada Sentence

En aaytu?

### Hindi Pronunciation

एन आय्तु?

### Meaning

Hindi: क्या हुआ?

English: What happened?

## Word-by-Word Breakdown

| Word | Hindi Meaning | English Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| En | क्या | what |
| aaytu | हो गया | happened/done |

## Sentence Construction Logic

This lesson extends the reusable pattern `___ aaytu?`. Keep the sentence short first, then add time/place/person words.

## Reusable Pattern

`___ aaytu?`

Examples:

- En aaytu?
- Yake late?
- Call miss aaytu.
- Booking cancel aaytu.
