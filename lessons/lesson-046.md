## Main Pattern

### Kannada Sentence

En aaytu?

### Hindi Pronunciation

एन आय्तु?

### Meaning

Hindi: क्या हुआ?

English: What happened?

## Word-by-Word Breakdown

| Word | Hindi Meaning | English Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| En | क्या | what |
| aaytu | हो गया | happened/done |

## Sentence Construction Logic

This lesson extends the reusable pattern `___ aaytu?`. Keep the sentence short first, then add time/place/person words.

## Reusable Pattern

`___ aaytu?`

Examples:

- En aaytu?
- Yake late?
- Call miss aaytu.
- Booking cancel aaytu.

## Guided Practice

## Production Practice

Say these in Kannada:

1. In a real Bangalore situation: What happened?
2. Change the place/person/item and say: Why late?
3. Use as the next line in a short conversation: Call missed.
4. Combine old knowledge with today: Booking got cancelled.
