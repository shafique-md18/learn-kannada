## Main Pattern

### Kannada Sentence

Traffic jaasti, late aaytu.

### Hindi Pronunciation

ट्रैफिक जास्ति, लेट आय्तु.

### Meaning

Hindi: ट्रैफिक ज्यादा था, देर हो गई.

English: Traffic was heavy, so it got late.

## Sentence Construction Logic

This lesson extends the reusable pattern `Reason, result`. Keep the sentence short first, then add time/place/person words.

## Reusable Pattern

`Reason, result`

Examples:

- Traffic jaasti, late aaytu.
- Adakke late aaytu.
- Reason enu?
- Sorry, late aaytu.
