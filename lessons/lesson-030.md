## Main Pattern

### Kannada Sentence

Naan illi irthini.

### Hindi Pronunciation

नान् इल्लि इर्तिनी.

### Meaning

Hindi: मैं यहां रहता हूं.

English: I live/stay here.

## Word-by-Word Breakdown

| Word | Hindi Meaning | English Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| naan | मैं | I |
| illi | यहां | here |
| irthini | रहता हूं | stay/live |

## Sentence Construction Logic

`irthini` means I stay/live/am there. Use it for where you live or are based.

## Reusable Pattern

`Naan ___ alli/illi irthini`

Examples:

- Naan illi irthini.
- Naan apartment alli irthini.
- Naan same floor alli.
- Naan illi new.

## Guided Practice
