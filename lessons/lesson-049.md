## Main Pattern

### Kannada Sentence

Nanage coffee ishta.

### Hindi Pronunciation

ननगे कॉफी इष्ट.

### Meaning

Hindi: मुझे कॉफी पसंद है.

English: I like coffee.

## Word-by-Word Breakdown

| Word | Hindi Meaning | English Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| Nanage | मुझे | to me/I |
| coffee | कॉफी | coffee |
| ishta | पसंद | like |

## Sentence Construction Logic

This lesson extends the reusable pattern `Nanage ___ ishta`. Keep the sentence short first, then add time/place/person words.

## Reusable Pattern

`Nanage ___ ishta`

Examples:

- Nanage coffee ishta.
- Nanage tea ishta illa.
- Tumba ishta.
- Food ishta.

## Guided Practice

Build these in Kannada:

1. I like coffee.
2. I do not like tea.
3. Like very much.
4. Like food.

## Production Practice

Say these in Kannada:

1. In a real Bangalore situation: I like coffee.
2. Change the place/person/item and say: I do not like tea.
3. Use as the next line in a short conversation: Like very much.
4. Combine old knowledge with today: Like food.
