## Main Pattern

### Kannada Sentence

Coffee ge barthira?

### Hindi Pronunciation

कॉफी गे बर्तिरा?

### Meaning

Hindi: कॉफी के लिए आएंगे?

English: Will you come for coffee?

## Word-by-Word Breakdown

| Word | Hindi Meaning | English Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| Coffee | कॉफी | coffee |
| ge | को/के लिए | to/for |
| barthira | आएंगे? | will you come? |

## Sentence Construction Logic

This lesson extends the reusable pattern `___ ge hogona/barthira?`. Keep the sentence short first, then add time/place/person words.

## Reusable Pattern

`___ ge hogona/barthira?`

Examples:

- Coffee ge barthira?
- Coffee ge hogona.
- Sanje free ideya?
- Together hogona.
