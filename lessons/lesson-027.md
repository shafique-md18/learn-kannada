## Main Pattern

### Kannada Sentence

Oota aayta?

### Hindi Pronunciation

ऊटा आय्ता?

### Meaning

Hindi: खाना हो गया?

English: Did you eat? / Is food done?

## Word-by-Word Breakdown

| Word | Hindi Meaning | English Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| oota | खाना | meal |
| aayta | हो गया? | done? |

## Sentence Construction Logic

`aayta?` asks if something is done. `Oota aayta?` is a common friendly question.

## Reusable Pattern

`___ aayta?`

Examples:

- Oota aayta?
- Payment aayta?
- Work aayta?
- Bill aayta?
