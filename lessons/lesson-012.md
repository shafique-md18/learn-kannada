## Main Pattern

### Kannada Sentence

Naan barthini.

### Hindi Pronunciation

नान् बर्तिनी.

### Meaning

Hindi: मैं आ रहा/रही हूं / मैं आऊंगा/आऊंगी.

English: I am coming / I will come.

## Word-by-Word Breakdown

| Word | Hindi Meaning | English Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| naan | मैं | I |
| barthini | आ रहा/रही हूं | I am coming / I will come |

## Sentence Construction Logic

For your own near-future action, use:

`Naan + action`

In spoken Kannada, the same form often works for "I am coming" and "I will come" depending on context.

## Reusable Pattern

`Naan ___` = I will/am ___

Examples:

- Naan barthini. = I am coming.
- Naan hogthini. = I am going.
- Naan madthini. = I will do it.

With time:

- Ivaga barthini. = I am coming now.
- Aamele barthini. = I will come later.
