# Lesson 025: My Name, Your Name

## Quick Recall

Say in Kannada:

1. Who are you?
2. Tell the name.
3. I am coming now.

## New Words

### Must Memorize

| Kannada | Hindi Pronunciation | Hindi Meaning | English Meaning | Bangalore Usage |

| nanna | नन्न | मेरा | my | Nanna name Rahul. |
| nimma | निम्म | आपका | your | Nimma name enu? |

## Main Pattern

### Kannada Sentence

Nimma name enu?

### Hindi Pronunciation

निम्म नेम एनु?

### Meaning

Hindi: आपका नाम क्या है?

English: What is your name?

## Word-by-Word Breakdown

| Word | Hindi Meaning | English Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| nimma | आपका | your |
| name | नाम | name |
| enu | क्या | what |

## Sentence Construction Logic

Use `nimma` for your and `nanna` for my. Put the thing first, then `enu` for what.

## Reusable Pattern

`Nimma ___ enu? / Nanna ___ ___`

Examples:

- Nimma name enu?
- Nanna name Rahul.
- Nimma kelasa enu?
- Nanna ooru Delhi.

## Guided Practice

Build these in Kannada:
