## Main Pattern

### Kannada Sentence

Meter alli barthira?

### Hindi Pronunciation

मीटर अल्लि बर्तिरा?

### Meaning

Hindi: मीटर पर आएंगे?

English: Will you come by meter?

## Word-by-Word Breakdown

| Word | Hindi Meaning | English Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| meter alli | मीटर पर | by meter |
| barthira | आएंगे? | will you come? |

## Sentence Construction Logic

Use `meter alli` for "by meter/on meter".

Then add `barthira?` to ask if the driver will come.

## Reusable Pattern

`___ alli barthira?` = Will you come by/on ___?

Examples:

- Meter alli barthira?
- Ivaga barthira?

Price:

- Yeshtu agutte? = How much will it be?
- Extra beda. = No extra.
- Fixed rate beda. = No fixed rate.
