## Main Pattern

### Kannada Sentence

Swalpa kaayiri.

### Hindi Pronunciation

स्वल्प कायिरि.

### Meaning

Hindi: थोड़ा इंतजार कीजिए.

English: Please wait a little.

## Word-by-Word Breakdown

| Word | Hindi Meaning | English Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| swalpa | थोड़ा | little/some |
| kaayiri | इंतजार कीजिए | wait |

## Sentence Construction Logic

`swalpa` softens requests. It is very useful in Bangalore Kannada.

`Swalpa kaayiri` sounds more polite than just `kaayiri`.

## Reusable Pattern

`Swalpa + action`

Examples:

- Swalpa kaayiri. = Wait a little.
- Swalpa banni. = Come a little/please come.
- Swalpa nodi. = Please look.

Other action lines:

- Illi koothkolli. = Sit here.
- Illi nilli. = Stop/stand here.
