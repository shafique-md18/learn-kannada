## Main Pattern

### Kannada Sentence

Neevu barthira?

### Hindi Pronunciation

नीवु बर्तिरा?

### Meaning

Hindi: क्या आप आएंगे?

English: Will you come?

## Word-by-Word Breakdown

| Word | Hindi Meaning | English Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| neevu | आप | you |
| barthira | आएंगे? | will come? |

## Sentence Construction Logic

For polite questions to another person, use:

`Neevu + action-question`

In daily speech, the question sound at the end matters.

## Reusable Pattern

`Neevu ___?` = Will you ___?

Examples:

- Neevu barthira? = Will you come?
- Neevu hogthira? = Will you go?
- Neevu madthira? = Will you do it?

Add time:

- Ivaga barthira? = Will you come now?
- Later barthira? = Will you come later?

## Guided Practice

Build these in Kannada:

1. Will you come?
2. Will you go there?
3. Will you do this?
4. Will you come now?

## Production Practice

Ask in Kannada:

1. To an auto driver: Will you come?
2. To maintenance: Will you come now?
3. To security: Will you go there?
4. To a shopkeeper: Will you give the bill?
