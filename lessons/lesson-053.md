## Main Pattern

### Kannada Sentence

Nanage tired aagide.

### Hindi Pronunciation

ननगे टायर्ड आगिदे.

### Meaning

Hindi: मैं थक गया हूं.

English: I am tired.

## Word-by-Word Breakdown

| Word | Hindi Meaning | English Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| Nanage | मुझे | to me |
| tired | थक गया | tired |
| aagide | हो गया है | is done |

## Sentence Construction Logic

This lesson extends the reusable pattern `Nanage ___`. Keep the sentence short first, then add time/place/person words.

## Reusable Pattern

Examples:
