## Main Pattern

### Kannada Sentence

Internet work agalla.

### Hindi Pronunciation

इंटरनेट वर्क आगल्ला.

### Meaning

Hindi: इंटरनेट काम नहीं कर रहा.

English: Internet is not working.

## Word-by-Word Breakdown

| Word | Hindi Meaning | English Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| Internet | इंटरनेट | internet |
| work | काम | work |
| agalla | नहीं होता | does not happen/work |

## Sentence Construction Logic

This lesson extends the reusable pattern `Utility + problem`. Keep the sentence short first, then add time/place/person words.

## Reusable Pattern

`Utility + problem`

Examples:

- Current illa.
- Water supply illa.
- Internet work agalla.
- Switch check madi.
