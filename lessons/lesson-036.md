## Main Pattern

### Kannada Sentence

Naale beligge barthini.

### Hindi Pronunciation

नाले बेळिग्गे बर्तिनी.

### Meaning

Hindi: मैं कल सुबह आऊंगा.

English: I will come tomorrow morning.

## Word-by-Word Breakdown

| Word | Hindi Meaning | English Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| Naale | कल | tomorrow |
| beligge | सुबह | morning |
| barthini | मैं आऊंगा | I will come |

## Sentence Construction Logic

This lesson extends the reusable pattern `Time + action`. Keep the sentence short first, then add time/place/person words.

## Reusable Pattern

`Time + action`

Examples:

- Naale beligge barthini.
- Sanje meet madi.
- Time confirm madi.
- Booking cancel madi.
