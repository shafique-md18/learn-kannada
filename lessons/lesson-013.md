## Main Pattern

### Kannada Sentence

Idannu kodi.

### Hindi Pronunciation

इदन्नु कोडि.

### Meaning

Hindi: इसे दीजिए.

English: Give this.

## Word-by-Word Breakdown

| Word | Hindi Meaning | English Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| idannu | इसे | this object |
| kodi | दीजिए | give |

## Sentence Construction Logic

Use `idu` for "this is". Use `idannu` when "this" is the object you are giving, taking, seeing, or buying.

`Idannu kodi` = Give this.

## Reusable Pattern

`Idannu ___` = Do ___ to this

Examples:

- Idannu kodi. = Give this.
- Idannu tagoli. = Take this.
- Idannu nodi. = Look at this.

Quantity:

- Ondu tea kodi. = Give one tea.
- Eradu coffee kodi. = Give two coffees.
