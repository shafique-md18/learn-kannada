## Main Pattern

### Kannada Sentence

Traffic jaasti.

### Hindi Pronunciation

ट्रैफिक जास्ति.

### Meaning

Hindi: ट्रैफिक ज्यादा है.

English: Traffic is heavy.

## Word-by-Word Breakdown

| Word | Hindi Meaning | English Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| traffic | ट्रैफिक | traffic |
| jaasti | ज्यादा | more/too much |

## Sentence Construction Logic

Use `jaasti` after a thing to say it is too much or heavy.

`Traffic jaasti` = traffic too much.

## Reusable Pattern

`___ jaasti` = ___ is too much/heavy

Examples:

- Traffic jaasti.
- Rate jaasti.
- Time jaasti.

Driving:

- Slow hogi. = Go slowly.
- Fast beda. = Not fast.
- Late agutte. = It will get late.

## Guided Practice

Build these in Kannada:

1. Traffic is heavy.
2. Go slowly.
3. Do not go fast.
4. It will get late.

## Production Practice

Say these in Kannada:

1. Tell the driver: traffic is heavy.
2. Tell the driver: go slowly.
3. Say the rate is too much.
4. Ask: how much time?

Say: Go slowly. Do not go fast. Traffic is heavy.
