## Main Pattern

### Kannada Sentence

Reach aadmele call madi.

### Hindi Pronunciation

रीच आद्मेले कॉल माडि.

### Meaning

Hindi: पहुंचने के बाद कॉल कीजिए.

English: Call after reaching.

## Word-by-Word Breakdown

| Word | Hindi Meaning | English Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| reach | पहुंचना | reach |
| aadmele | होने के बाद | after |
| call madi | कॉल कीजिए | call |

## Sentence Construction Logic

`aadmele` means "after something happens".

Use it after an action:

`Reach aadmele` = after reaching.

## Reusable Pattern

`___ aadmele ___ madi` = After ___, do ___

Examples:

- Reach aadmele call madi.
- Pickup aadmele call madi.

Transport combo lines:

- Wait madi.
- Confirm madi.
- Meter okay na?
