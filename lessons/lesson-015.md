## Main Pattern

### Kannada Sentence

Innu beku.

### Hindi Pronunciation

इन्नु बेकु.

### Meaning

Hindi: और चाहिए.

English: I want more.

## Word-by-Word Breakdown

| Word | Hindi Meaning | English Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| innu | और | more |
| beku | चाहिए | want/need |

## Sentence Construction Logic

Use `innu` before `beku` when you want more.

Use `saaku` alone when something is enough.

## Reusable Pattern

`Innu ___ beku` = I want more ___

Examples:

- Innu water beku.
- Innu coffee beku.
- Innu packet beku.

`Swalpa kadime madi` = Make it a little less.

Examples:

- Swalpa kadime madi.
- Hechchu beda.
- Saaku.

## Guided Practice

Build these in Kannada:

1. I want more water.
2. Enough.
3. Make it a little less.
4. I want a small packet.

## Production Practice

Say these in Kannada:

1. You want more tea.
2. The shopkeeper gives too much; say enough.
3. Ask for a big packet.
4. Tell them you do not want more.

## Real-Life Bangalore Roleplay

At a tea stall, they pour sugar or milk. You want less.

Say: Make it a little less. Enough.
