## Main Pattern

### Kannada Sentence

Avru yaaru?

### Hindi Pronunciation

अव्रु यारु?

### Meaning

Hindi: वे कौन हैं?

English: Who is he/she? / Who are they?

## Word-by-Word Breakdown

| Word | Hindi Meaning | English Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| avru | वे / वह आदर से | he/she/they respectfully |
| yaaru | कौन | who |

## Sentence Construction Logic

Use `yaaru` for "who".

Use `avru` as a respectful spoken form for he, she, or they. It is very useful in Bangalore because it sounds polite.

## Reusable Pattern

`___ yaaru?` = Who is ___?

Examples:

- Avru yaaru?
- Neevu yaaru?

Identity:

- Naan security alla. = I am not security.
- Avru owner. = He/she is owner.
