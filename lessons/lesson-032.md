## Main Pattern

### Kannada Sentence

Yaava time barthira?

### Hindi Pronunciation

याव टाइम बर्तिरा?

### Meaning

Hindi: किस टाइम आएंगे?

English: At what time will you come?

## Word-by-Word Breakdown

| Word | Hindi Meaning | English Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| Yaava | कौन सा | which |
| time | किस समय | what time |
| barthira | आएंगे? | will you come? |

## Sentence Construction Logic

This lesson extends the reusable pattern `Yaava time + action?`. Keep the sentence short first, then add time/place/person words.

## Reusable Pattern

`Yaava time + action?`

Examples:

- Yaava time barthira?
- Three ghante barthini.
- Half hour alli barthini.
- Late agutte.

## Guided Practice

Build these in Kannada:

1. What time will you come?
2. I will come at three.
3. I will come in half hour.
4. It will be late.

## Production Practice

Say these in Kannada:

1. In a real Bangalore situation: What time will you come?
2. Change the place/person/item and say: I will come at three.
3. Use as the next line in a short conversation: I will come in half hour.
4. Combine old knowledge with today: It will be late.
