## Main Pattern

### Kannada Sentence

Location kalisi.

### Hindi Pronunciation

लोकेशन कळिसि.

### Meaning

Hindi: लोकेशन भेजिए.

English: Send the location.

## Word-by-Word Breakdown

| Word | Hindi Meaning | English Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| location | लोकेशन | location |
| kalisi | भेजिए | send |

## Sentence Construction Logic

`kalisi` means send. Put the thing to send before it.

Pickup and drop are commonly used in English in Bangalore transport conversations.

## Reusable Pattern

`___ kalisi` = Send ___

Examples:

- Location kalisi.
- Number kalisi.

Transport lines:

- Pickup illi.
- Drop office alli.
- Cab bandide.
- Location tappu.

## Guided Practice

Build these in Kannada:

1. Send location.
2. Pickup is here.
3. Drop is at office.
4. The cab has arrived.

## Production Practice

Say these in Kannada:

1. Tell the cab driver: pickup is here.
2. Ask the driver to send location.
3. Say the location is wrong.
4. Say drop is at the gate.

## Real-Life Bangalore Roleplay

Cab driver calls and says he cannot find the pickup.

Tell him:

Pickup is here. Send location. I am outside the gate.

Use Kannada.
