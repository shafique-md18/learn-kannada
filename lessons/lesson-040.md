## Main Pattern

### Kannada Sentence

Key tagondu banni.

### Hindi Pronunciation

की तगोंडु बन्नि.

### Meaning

Hindi: चाबी लेकर आइए.

English: Bring the key.

## Word-by-Word Breakdown

| Word | Hindi Meaning | English Meaning |

| Key | चाबी | key |
| tagondu | लेकर आइए | bring |
| banni | आइए | come |

## Sentence Construction Logic

This lesson extends the reusable pattern `___ tagondu banni`. Keep the sentence short first, then add time/place/person words.

## Reusable Pattern

`___ tagondu banni`

Examples:

- Key tagondu banni.
- Photo kalisi.
- Problem heli.
- Bill tagondu banni.
