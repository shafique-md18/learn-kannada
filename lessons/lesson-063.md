## Main Pattern

### Kannada Sentence

Ide, togoli.

### Hindi Pronunciation

इदे, तगोळि.

### Meaning

Hindi: है, ले लीजिए.

English: It is there, take it.

## Word-by-Word Breakdown

| Word | Hindi Meaning | English Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| Ide | है | is there |
| togoli | ले लीजिए | take it |

## Sentence Construction Logic

This lesson extends the reusable pattern `Listen for reply chunks`. Keep the sentence short first, then add time/place/person words.

## Reusable Pattern

`Listen for reply chunks`

Examples:

- Ide, togoli.
- Illa saar.
- Naale banni.
- Cash only.
