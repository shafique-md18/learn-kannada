## Main Pattern

### Kannada Sentence

Majestic hogbeku.

### Hindi Pronunciation

मजेस्टिक होग्बेकु.

### Meaning

Hindi: मजेस्टिक जाना है.

English: I need to go to Majestic.

## Word-by-Word Breakdown

| Word | Hindi Meaning | English Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| Majestic | मजेस्टिक | Majestic |
| hogbeku | जाना है | need to go |

## Sentence Construction Logic

Use `Place + hogbeku` when you need to go somewhere.

This is one of the most useful travel patterns.

## Reusable Pattern

`___ hogbeku` = I need to go to ___

Examples:

- Office hogbeku.
- Metro hogbeku.
- Bengaluru hogbeku.

`___ barbeku` = need to come to ___

Examples:

- Illi barbeku.
- Gate horage barbeku.
