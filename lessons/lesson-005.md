## Main Pattern

### Kannada Sentence

Office alli.

### Hindi Pronunciation

ऑफिस अल्लि.

### Meaning

Hindi: ऑफिस में / ऑफिस वहां है.

English: In the office / The office is there.

## Word-by-Word Breakdown

| Word | Hindi Meaning | English Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| office | ऑफिस | office |
| alli | में / वहां | in/there |

## Sentence Construction Logic

`alli` can mean "there" or "in/at" depending on context.

For place phrases, use:

`Place + alli` = in/at that place.

## Reusable Pattern

`___ alli` = in/at ___

Examples:

- Bengaluru alli.
- Office alli.
- Apartment alli.

Inside/outside:

- Room olage. = Inside the room.
- Gate horage. = Outside the gate.
