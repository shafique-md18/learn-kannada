## Main Pattern

### Kannada Sentence

Lift elli?

### Hindi Pronunciation

लिफ्ट एल्ली?

### Meaning

Hindi: लिफ्ट कहां है?

English: Where is the lift?

## Word-by-Word Breakdown

| Word | Hindi Meaning | English Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| lift | लिफ्ट | lift |
| elli | कहां | where |

## Sentence Construction Logic

For "Where is ___?", put the thing/place first, then `elli`.

`Lift elli?` literally means "Lift where?"

## Reusable Pattern

`___ elli?` = Where is ___?

Examples:

- Office elli?
- Room elli?
- Washroom elli?

Replies:

- Illi. = Here.
- Alli. = There.
- Office alli. = Office is there.

## Guided Practice

Build these in Kannada:

1. Where is the office?
2. Where is the room?
3. The lift is there.
4. Water is here.

## Production Practice

Ask in Kannada:

1. Where is the shop?
2. Where is the washroom?
3. Where is the bill?
4. Say: Home is there.

## Real-Life Bangalore Roleplay

You enter an office building. Ask the security guard where the lift is.

Guard replies: Alli.

You say okay.

Write both your lines in Kannada.
