## Main Pattern

### Kannada Sentence

List alli idu ideya?

### Hindi Pronunciation

लिस्ट अल्लि इदु इदेया?

### Meaning

Hindi: लिस्ट में यह है क्या?

English: Is this on the list?

## Word-by-Word Breakdown

| Word | Hindi Meaning | English Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| List | सूची | list |
| alli | वहां/में | there/in |
| idu | यह | this |
| ideya | है क्या? | is there? |

## Sentence Construction Logic

This lesson extends the reusable pattern `Shopping sequence`. Keep the sentence short first, then add time/place/person words.

## Reusable Pattern

`Shopping sequence`

Examples:

- Milk ideya?
- Bread kodi.
- Eggs beku.
- Total eshtu?

## Guided Practice

Build these in Kannada:

1. Is milk available?
2. Give bread.
3. Want eggs.
4. Total how much?

## Production Practice

Say these in Kannada:

2. Change the place/person/item and say: Give bread.
3. Use as the next line in a short conversation: Want eggs.
4. Combine old knowledge with today: Total how much?
