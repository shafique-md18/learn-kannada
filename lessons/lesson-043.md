## Main Pattern

### Kannada Sentence

Naan office ge hode.

### Hindi Pronunciation

नान् ऑफिस गे होदे.

### Meaning

Hindi: मैं ऑफिस गया.

English: I went to office.

## Word-by-Word Breakdown

| Word | Hindi Meaning | English Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| Naan | मैं | I |
| office | ऑफिस | office |
| ge | को/के लिए | to/for |
| hode | मैं गया | I went |

## Sentence Construction Logic

This lesson extends the reusable pattern `Naan ___ ge hode / Naan ___ bande`. Keep the sentence short first, then add time/place/person words.

## Reusable Pattern

`Naan ___ ge hode / Naan ___ bande`

Examples:

- Naan office ge hode.
- Naan manege hode.
- Ninne bande.
- Naale barthini.
