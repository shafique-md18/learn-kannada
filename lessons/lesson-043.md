## Main Pattern

### Kannada Sentence

Naan office hogidde.

### Hindi Pronunciation

नान् ऑफिस होगिद्दे.

### Meaning

Hindi: मैं ऑफिस गया था.

English: I went to office.

## Word-by-Word Breakdown

| Word | Hindi Meaning | English Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| Naan | मैं | I |
| office | ऑफिस | office |
| hogidde | गया था | went |

## Sentence Construction Logic

This lesson extends the reusable pattern `Naan ___ hogidde`. Keep the sentence short first, then add time/place/person words.

## Reusable Pattern

`Naan ___ hogidde`

Examples:

- Naan officege hogidde.
- Naan manege hogidde.
- Ninne bandide.
- Naale barthini.
