## Main Pattern

### Kannada Sentence

Kannada swalpa barutte.

### Hindi Pronunciation

कन्नडा स्वल्प बरुत्ते.

### Meaning

Hindi: थोड़ी कन्नड़ आती है.

English: I know a little Kannada.

## Word-by-Word Breakdown

| Word | Hindi Meaning | English Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| Kannada | कन्नड़ | Kannada |
| swalpa | थोड़ा | little/please |
| barutte | आता है | know/comes |

## Sentence Construction Logic

This lesson extends the reusable pattern `Language + barutte/baralla`. Keep the sentence short first, then add time/place/person words.

## Reusable Pattern

`Language + barutte/baralla`

Examples:

- Kannada swalpa barutte.
- Hindi barutte.
- English barutte.
- Kannada kalithidini.

## Guided Practice

Build these in Kannada:

1. I know little Kannada.
2. I know Hindi.
3. I know English.
4. I am learning Kannada.

## Production Practice

Say these in Kannada:

1. In a real Bangalore situation: I know little Kannada.
2. Change the place/person/item and say: I know Hindi.
3. Use as the next line in a short conversation: I know English.
4. Combine old knowledge with today: I am learning Kannada.

Tell a shopkeeper you are learning Kannada and ask them to speak slowly.
