## Main Pattern

### Kannada Sentence

Illi banni.

### Hindi Pronunciation

इल्लि बन्नि.

### Meaning

Hindi: यहां आइए.

English: Please come here.

## Word-by-Word Breakdown

| Word | Hindi Meaning | English Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| illi | यहां | here |
| banni | आइए | come |

## Sentence Construction Logic

For polite commands, use the action word directly.

Place first, action second:

`Illi banni` = here come.

## Reusable Pattern

`Place + action`

Examples:

- Illi banni. = Come here.
- Alli hogi. = Go there.
- Idu nodi. = Look at this.
- Bill kodi. = Give the bill.
- Idu tagoli. = Take this.
