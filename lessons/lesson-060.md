## Main Pattern

### Kannada Sentence

Bangalore hege ide?

### Hindi Pronunciation

बैंगलोर हेगे इदे?

### Meaning

Hindi: बंगलौर कैसा है?

English: How is Bangalore?

## Word-by-Word Breakdown

| Word | Hindi Meaning | English Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| Bangalore | बंगलौर | Bangalore |
| hege | कैसा | how |
| ide | है | is there |

## Sentence Construction Logic

This lesson extends the reusable pattern `___ hege ide?`. Keep the sentence short first, then add time/place/person words.

## Reusable Pattern

`___ hege ide?`

Examples:

- Bangalore hege ide?
- Weather chennagide.
- Area safe ide.
- Bangalore expensive ide.

## Guided Practice

## Production Practice

Say these in Kannada:

2. Change the place/person/item and say: Weather is good.
3. Use as the next line in a short conversation: Area is safe.
4. Combine old knowledge with today: Bangalore is expensive.
