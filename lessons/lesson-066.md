## Sentence Construction Logic

This lesson extends the reusable pattern `Natural variants`. Keep the sentence short first, then add time/place/person words.

## Reusable Pattern

`Natural variants`

Examples:

- Ondu nimisha kaayiri.
- Cab bartha ide.
- Payment madbeku.
- Sari sari.

## Guided Practice

Build these in Kannada:

1. Wait one minute.
2. Cab is coming.

## Production Practice

Say these in Kannada:

1. In a real Bangalore situation: Wait one minute.
2. Change the place/person/item and say: Cab is coming.
3. Use as the next line in a short conversation: Need to pay.
4. Combine old knowledge with today: Okay okay.
