## Main Pattern

### Kannada Sentence

Neevu enu madthidira?

### Hindi Pronunciation

नीवु एनु मद्तिदिरा?

### Meaning

Hindi: आप क्या कर रहे हैं?

English: What are you doing?

## Word-by-Word Breakdown

| Word | Hindi Meaning | English Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| neevu | आप | you |
| enu | क्या | what |
| madthidira | कर रहे हैं | are doing |

## Sentence Construction Logic

`madthira` asks about what someone does generally. `madthidira` asks what they are doing now.

## Reusable Pattern

`Neevu enu madthidira?`

Examples:

- Neevu enu madthidira?
- Naan work madthini.
- Naan study madthini.
- Naan IT alli work madthini.

## Guided Practice

Build these in Kannada:

1. What are you doing?
2. I work.
3. I study.
4. I work in IT.

## Production Practice

Say these in Kannada:

1. Ask a colleague what they are doing.
2. Say you work.
3. Say you work in office.
4. Ask what work they do.

## Real-Life Bangalore Roleplay

A neighbour asks what you do. Answer simply and ask them back.
