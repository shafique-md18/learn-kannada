## Main Pattern

### Kannada Sentence

Rate eshtu?

### Hindi Pronunciation

रेट एष्टु?

### Meaning

Hindi: दाम कितना है?

English: How much is the price?

## Word-by-Word Breakdown

| Word | Hindi Meaning | English Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| rate | दाम | rate/price |
| eshtu | कितना | how much |

## Sentence Construction Logic

Put the thing you are asking about first, then `eshtu`.

`Rate eshtu?` = Price how much?

## Reusable Pattern

`___ eshtu?` = How much/how many ___?

Examples:

- Rate eshtu?
- Bill eshtu?
- Idannu eshtu?

`___ ideya?` = Is there ___?

Examples:

- Change ideya?
- Water ideya?

## Guided Practice

Build these in Kannada:

1. How much is the bill?
2. How much is this?
3. Is there change?
4. Is there water?

## Production Practice

Say these in Kannada:
