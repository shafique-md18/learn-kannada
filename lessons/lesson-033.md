## Main Pattern

### Kannada Sentence

Yaavaga barthira?

### Hindi Pronunciation

यावाग बर्तिरा?

### Meaning

Hindi: कब आएंगे?

English: When will you come?

## Word-by-Word Breakdown

| Word | Hindi Meaning | English Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| Yaavaga | कब | when |
| barthira | आएंगे? | will you come? |

## Sentence Construction Logic

This lesson extends the reusable pattern `Yaavaga ___?`. Keep the sentence short first, then add time/place/person words.

## Reusable Pattern

`Yaavaga ___?`

Examples:

- Yaavaga barthira?
- Iga banni.
- Amele madthini.
- Munche call madi.

## Guided Practice

Build these in Kannada:

1. When will you come?
2. Come now.
3. I will do later.
4. Call before.

## Production Practice

Say these in Kannada:

1. In a real Bangalore situation: When will you come?
2. Change the place/person/item and say: Come now.
3. Use as the next line in a short conversation: I will do later.
4. Combine old knowledge with today: Call before.

## Real-Life Bangalore Roleplay

Ask a delivery agent when he will come, then say call before coming.
