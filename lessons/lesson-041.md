## Main Pattern

### Kannada Sentence

Parcel bandilla.

### Hindi Pronunciation

पार्सल बन्दिल्ला.

### Meaning

Hindi: पार्सल नहीं आया.

English: Parcel has not arrived.

## Word-by-Word Breakdown

| Word | Hindi Meaning | English Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| Parcel | पार्सल | parcel |
| bandilla | नहीं आया | has not arrived |

## Sentence Construction Logic

This lesson extends the reusable pattern `___ bandide / bandilla`. Keep the sentence short first, then add time/place/person words.

## Reusable Pattern

`___ bandide / bandilla`

Examples:

- Parcel bandide.
- Parcel bandilla.
- Wrong item bandide.
- Return madi.
