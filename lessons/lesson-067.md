## Main Pattern

### Kannada Sentence

Koramangala hogbeku.

### Hindi Pronunciation

कोरमंगला होग्बेकु.

### Meaning

Hindi: कोरमंगला जाना है.

English: Need to go to Koramangala.

## Word-by-Word Breakdown

## Sentence Construction Logic

This lesson extends the reusable pattern `Full ride chunks`. Keep the sentence short first, then add time/place/person words.

## Reusable Pattern

`Full ride chunks`

Examples:

- Meter only.
- Signal alli left.
- Gate alli drop madi.
