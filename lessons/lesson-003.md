## Main Pattern

### Kannada Sentence

Idu office alla.

### Hindi Pronunciation

इदु ऑफिस अल्ला.

### Meaning

Hindi: यह ऑफिस नहीं है.

English: This is not office.

## Word-by-Word Breakdown

| Word | Hindi Meaning | English Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| idu | यह | this |
| office | ऑफिस | office |
| alla | नहीं है | is not |

## Sentence Construction Logic

For simple "is not" sentences, put `alla` at the end.

`Idu office alla` = This office not.

## Reusable Pattern

`Idu ___ alla` = This is not ___

Examples:

- Idu shop alla.
- Idu water alla.
- Idu mane alla.

Short replies:

- Haudu. = Yes.
- Illa. = No.
- Sari. = Okay.
- Paravagilla. = No problem.

## Guided Practice

## Production Practice

Say these in Kannada:

1. Someone points to a shop, but it is an office.
2. Someone offers coffee, but you do not want it.
3. The guard says "wait"; you say okay.
4. Someone apologizes; say no problem.

## Real-Life Bangalore Roleplay

Security guard: Idu office?

It is not office. It is a shop.

Reply in Kannada using two short sentences.
