## Main Pattern

### Kannada Sentence

Technician kalisi.

### Hindi Pronunciation

टेक्नीशियन कळिसि.

### Meaning

Hindi: टेक्नीशियन भेजिए.

English: Send a technician.

## Sentence Construction Logic

This lesson extends the reusable pattern `Person + kalisi`. Keep the sentence short first, then add time/place/person words.

## Reusable Pattern

`Person + kalisi`

Examples:

- Technician kalisi.
- Plumber beku.
- Electrician kalisi.
- Slot ideya?

## Guided Practice
