## Main Pattern

### Kannada Sentence

Nimma family illi ideya?

### Hindi Pronunciation

निम्म फैमिली इल्लि इदेया?

### Meaning

Hindi: आपकी फैमिली यहां है क्या?

English: Is your family here?

## Word-by-Word Breakdown

## Sentence Construction Logic

This lesson extends the reusable pattern `Nimma family ___?`. Keep the sentence short first, then add time/place/person words.

## Reusable Pattern

`Nimma family ___?`

Examples:

- Nimma family illi ideya?
- Parents Delhi alli.
- Kids school alli.
- Family Bangalore alli.

## Guided Practice
