## Main Pattern

### Kannada Sentence

Daily office hogthini.

### Hindi Pronunciation

डेली ऑफिस होग्तिनी.

### Meaning

Hindi: मैं रोज़ ऑफिस जाता हूं.

English: I go to office daily.

## Sentence Construction Logic

This lesson extends the reusable pattern `Frequency + action`. Keep the sentence short first, then add time/place/person words.

## Reusable Pattern

`Frequency + action`

Examples:

- Daily office hogthini.
- Sometimes metro hogthini.
- Usually cab beda.
- Weekend mane alli.
