## Main Pattern

### Kannada Sentence

Daily office hogthini.

### Hindi Pronunciation

डेली ऑफिस होग्तिनी.

### Meaning

Hindi: मैं रोज़ ऑफिस जाता हूं.

English: I go to office daily.

## Sentence Construction Logic

This lesson extends the reusable pattern `Frequency + action`. Keep the sentence short first, then add time/place/person words.

## Reusable Pattern

`Frequency + action`

Examples:

- Daily office hogthini.
- Sometimes metro hogthini.
- Usually cab beda.
- Weekend mane alli.

## Guided Practice

Build these in Kannada:

1. I go to office daily.
2. Sometimes I go by metro.
3. Usually cab not wanted.
4. Weekend at home.

## Production Practice

Say these in Kannada:

1. In a real Bangalore situation: I go to office daily.
2. Change the place/person/item and say: Sometimes I go by metro.
3. Use as the next line in a short conversation: Usually cab not wanted.
4. Combine old knowledge with today: Weekend at home.
