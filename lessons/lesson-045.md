## Main Pattern

### Kannada Sentence

Naan avru nodide.

### Hindi Pronunciation

नान् अव्रु नोडिदे.

### Meaning

Hindi: मैंने उन्हें देखा.

English: I saw him/her.

## Word-by-Word Breakdown

| Word | Hindi Meaning | English Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| Naan | मैं | I |
| avru | वे/वह आदर से | he/she respectfully |
| nodide | देखा | saw |

## Sentence Construction Logic

This lesson extends the reusable pattern `Naan ___ madide/helide`. Keep the sentence short first, then add time/place/person words.

## Reusable Pattern

`Naan ___ madide/helide`

Examples:

- Naan call madide.
- Naan message madide.
- Naan helide.
- Reply illa.
