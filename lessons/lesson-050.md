## Main Pattern

### Kannada Sentence

Coffee chennagide.

### Hindi Pronunciation

कॉफी चेन्नागिदे.

### Meaning

Hindi: कॉफी अच्छी है.

English: Coffee is good.

## Word-by-Word Breakdown

| Word | Hindi Meaning | English Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| Coffee | कॉफी | coffee |
| chennagide | अच्छा है | is good |

## Sentence Construction Logic

This lesson extends the reusable pattern `___ chennagide`. Keep the sentence short first, then add time/place/person words.

## Reusable Pattern

`___ chennagide`

Examples:

- Coffee chennagide.
- Idu chennagilla.
- Ruchi chennagide.
- Spicy jaasti.

## Guided Practice

Build these in Kannada:

1. Coffee is good.
2. This is not good.
3. Taste is good.
4. Too spicy.

## Production Practice

Say these in Kannada:

1. In a real Bangalore situation: Coffee is good.
2. Change the place/person/item and say: This is not good.
3. Use as the next line in a short conversation: Taste is good.
4. Combine old knowledge with today: Too spicy.

## Real-Life Bangalore Roleplay

At a restaurant, say the food is good but too spicy.
