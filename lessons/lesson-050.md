## Main Pattern

### Kannada Sentence

Coffee chennagide.

### Hindi Pronunciation

कॉफी चेन्नागिदे.

### Meaning

Hindi: कॉफी अच्छी है.

English: Coffee is good.

## Word-by-Word Breakdown

| Word | Hindi Meaning | English Meaning |
|---|---|---|

## Sentence Construction Logic

This lesson extends the reusable pattern `___ chennagide`. Keep the sentence short first, then add time/place/person words.

## Reusable Pattern

`___ chennagide`

Examples:

- Coffee chennagide.
- Idu chennagilla.
- Ruchi chennagide.
- Spicy jaasti.
