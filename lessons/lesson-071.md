## Main Pattern

### Kannada Sentence

Menu kodi.

### Hindi Pronunciation

मेन्यू कोडि.

### Meaning

Hindi: मेन्यू दीजिए.

English: Give the menu.

## Sentence Construction Logic

This lesson extends the reusable pattern `Restaurant sequence`. Keep the sentence short first, then add time/place/person words.

## Reusable Pattern

`Restaurant sequence`

Examples:

- Menu kodi.
- Veg ideya?
- Less spicy madi.
- Parcel madi.

## Guided Practice

Build these in Kannada:

2. Is veg available?
3. Make less spicy.
4. Pack parcel.

4. Combine old knowledge with today: Pack parcel.
