## Main Pattern

### Kannada Sentence

Menu kodi.

### Hindi Pronunciation

मेन्यू कोडि.

### Meaning

Hindi: मेन्यू दीजिए.

English: Give the menu.

## Word-by-Word Breakdown

## Sentence Construction Logic

This lesson extends the reusable pattern `Restaurant sequence`. Keep the sentence short first, then add time/place/person words.

## Reusable Pattern

`Restaurant sequence`

Examples:

- Parcel madi.
