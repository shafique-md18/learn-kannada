## Main Pattern

### Kannada Sentence

Kannada swalpa kashta.

### Hindi Pronunciation

कन्नडा स्वल्प कष्ट.

### Meaning

Hindi: कन्नड़ थोड़ा कठिन है.

English: Kannada is a little difficult.

## Word-by-Word Breakdown

| Word | Hindi Meaning | English Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| Kannada | कन्नड़ | Kannada |
| swalpa | थोड़ा | little/please |
| kashta | मुश्किल | difficult |

## Sentence Construction Logic

This lesson extends the reusable pattern `___ sulabha/kashta`. Keep the sentence short first, then add time/place/person words.

## Reusable Pattern

`___ sulabha/kashta`

Examples:

- Idu sulabha.
- Kannada swalpa kashta.
- Artha aaytu.
- Slow aagi heli.
