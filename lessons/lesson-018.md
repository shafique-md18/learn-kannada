## Main Pattern

### Kannada Sentence

Idannu togothini.

### Hindi Pronunciation

इदन्नु तगोतीनि.

### Meaning

Hindi: मैं इसे लेता/लेती हूं.

English: I will take this.

## Word-by-Word Breakdown

| Word | Hindi Meaning | English Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| idannu | इसे | this object |
| togothini | मैं लेता/लेती हूं | I will take |

## Sentence Construction Logic

Use `togothini` when you decide to buy/take something.

`Idannu togothini` = I will take this.

## Reusable Pattern

`___ togothini` = I will take ___

Examples:

- Idannu togothini.
- Adannu togothini.
- Ondu packet togothini.

Useful shop lines:

- Ella saaku. = All enough / That is all.
- Mathe bill kodi. = Then give bill.
- Idu sari na? = This is okay, right?

## Guided Practice
