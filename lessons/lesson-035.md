## Main Pattern

### Kannada Sentence

Payment aagide.

### Hindi Pronunciation

पेमेंट आगिदे.

### Meaning

Hindi: पेमेंट हो गया है.

English: Payment is done.

## Word-by-Word Breakdown

| Word | Hindi Meaning | English Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| Payment | पेमेंट | payment |
| aagide | हो गया है | is done/has happened |

## Sentence Construction Logic

This lesson extends the reusable pattern `___ aagide / ___ aagilla`. Keep the sentence short first, then add time/place/person words.

## Reusable Pattern

`___ aagide / ___ aagilla`

Examples:

- Payment aagide.
- Work mugitu.
- Innuu illa.
- Work finish aagilla.
