## Main Pattern

### Kannada Sentence

Straight hogi.

### Hindi Pronunciation

स्ट्रेट होगि.

### Meaning

Hindi: सीधा जाइए.

English: Go straight.

## Word-by-Word Breakdown

| Word | Hindi Meaning | English Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| straight | सीधा | straight |
| hogi | जाइए | go |

## Sentence Construction Logic

For directions, spoken Bangalore Kannada often uses English direction words with Kannada actions.

This is natural daily speech:

- Left hogi.
- Right hogi.
- Straight hogi.

Use `madi` after action-like English words:

- Stop madi.
- Left turn madi.

## Reusable Pattern

`Direction + hogi` = Go ___

Examples:

- Left hogi.
- Right hogi.
- Straight hogi.

`Action + madi` = Do ___

Examples:

- Stop madi.
- Left turn madi.
- Right turn madi.
