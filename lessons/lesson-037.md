## Main Pattern

### Kannada Sentence

Fan work agalla.

### Hindi Pronunciation

फैन वर्क आगल्ला.

### Meaning

Hindi: पंखा काम नहीं कर रहा.

English: The fan is not working.

## Word-by-Word Breakdown

| Word | Hindi Meaning | English Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| Fan | पंखा | fan |
| work | काम | work |
| agalla | नहीं होता | does not happen/work |

## Sentence Construction Logic

This lesson extends the reusable pattern `___ agalla`. Keep the sentence short first, then add time/place/person words.

## Reusable Pattern

`___ agalla`

Examples:

- Fan work agalla.
- Door open agalla.
- Door close agalla.
- Problem ide.
