# Survival Dialogues

This file collects short practical Bangalore dialogues for revision.

## Tea Stall

You: Coffee beku.

Stall person: Tea?

You: Tea beda. Coffee beku.

Stall person: Sari.

## Shop Bill

You: Bill kodi.

Shopkeeper: Idu bill.

You: Sari.

## Office Building

You: Lift elli?

Security: Alli.

You: Sari.

## Apartment Gate

You: Avru yaaru?

Security: Avru owner.

You: Sari.

## Delivery Call

Delivery agent: Neevu barthira?

You: Haudu, ivaga barthini. Swalpa kaayiri.

## Finding A Room

You: Room elli?

Security: Room olage.

You: Room torisi.

## Auto Direction

You: Straight hogi.

Driver: Illi?

You: Left turn madi. Illi stop madi.

## Maintenance Call

You: Ivaga barthira?

Maintenance: Ivaga agalla.

You: Sari. Later barthira?

## Grocery Purchase

You: Idannu eshtu?

Shopkeeper: Thirty.

You: Idannu togothini. Bag beku.

Shopkeeper: UPI?

You: Haudu, payment madthini.

## Asking Availability

You: Colgate ideya?

Shopkeeper: Ide.

You: Ondu kodi. Receipt kodi.

## Price Too High

You: Rate eshtu?

Shopkeeper: Hundred.

You: Rate jaasti. Swalpa kadime madi.

## Auto Meter

You: Meter alli barthira?

Driver: Elli hogbeku?

You: Office hogbeku. Extra beda.

## Cab Pickup

Driver: Pickup elli?

You: Pickup gate horage. Location kalisi.

Driver: Reach aadmele call madthini.

You: Sari.

## Route

You: Route gotha?

Driver: Gotha.

You: Traffic jaasti andre, main road alli hogi.


## Neighbour Intro

You: Nanna name Shafique. Naan illi irthini.

Neighbour: Nimma ooru elli?

You: Naan Delhi inda. Kannada kalithidini.

## Maintenance Problem

You: Fan work agalla. Idu check madi.

Staff: Ivaga busy.

You: Sari. Yaava time barthira?

## Delivery Problem

You: Order wrong address ge hogide.

Agent: Location kalisi.

You: Correct address kalisthini. Reschedule madi.

## Social Invite

You: Sanje free ideya?

Friend: Haudu.

You: Coffee ge hogona?

## Clarification

Speaker: Fast Kannada line.

You: Artha agilla. Innondu sala slow aagi heli.
