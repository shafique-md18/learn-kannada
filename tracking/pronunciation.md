# Pronunciation Notes

This file will collect pronunciation rules for Hindi speakers learning spoken Bangalore Kannada.

No Kannada script will be used.

## Core Notes

## Lessons 001-012

### Short Final `u`

Many Kannada words end with a short `u` sound. Do not make it too heavy.

Examples:

- idu: इदु
- adu: अदु
- beku: बेकु
- yaaru: यारु

### Double `ll`

`ll` in transliteration is held slightly stronger than a normal Hindi ल.

Examples:

- illi: इल्लि
- alli: अल्लि
- illa: इल्ला

### `ḷ`-Like Sound Written As `l` Or `ळ`

Some words have a heavier retroflex sound. Hindi pronunciation support may show ळ.

Examples:

- tagoli: तगोळि
- koothkolli: कूत्कोळ्ळि
- heli: हेळि

You do not need perfection at first. Aim to be understandable.

### Natural English Loan Words

Daily Bangalore Kannada commonly uses English words for modern objects and directions.

Examples:

- office
- bill
- lift
- room
- gate
- left
- right
- straight
- stop

Use these naturally. Do not hunt for obscure Kannada replacements in daily speech.

## Lessons 013-024

### `dd` And `tt` Feel Stronger

Some words have a stronger stop sound. Say them clearly but do not over-stress.

Examples:

- duddu: दुद्दु
- dodda: दोड्ड
- tappu: तप्पु

### `gtha` / `dtha` Clusters

Spoken forms like `hogthira`, `madthini`, and `togothini` may feel fast.

Practice slowly first:

- hog-thi-ra: होग्तिरा
- mad-thi-ni: मद्तिनी
- togo-thi-ni: तगोतीनि

### Common Mixed Transport Speech

Bangalore transport speech often keeps English travel words:

- pickup
- drop
- location
- route
- traffic
- meter
- shortcut

This is natural daily speech, not a shortcut in the bad sense.


## Lessons 025-072

### Common Fast Spoken Endings

- `madthini`: मद्तिनी
- `madthira`: मद्तिरा
- `hode`: होदे
- `bande`: बन्दे
- `konde`: कोंडे
- `aaytu`: आय्तु
- `agalla`: आगल्ला

### Useful Listening Chunks

Practice these as whole sounds:

- En aaytu?
- Artha agilla.
- Innondu sala heli.
- Swalpa time beku.
- Beda bidi.
- Kannada swalpa barutte.
