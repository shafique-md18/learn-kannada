# Reusable Sentence Patterns

This file collects every reusable sentence pattern from the course.

## Questions

### Idu enu?

Meaning: What is this?

Pattern: `Idu enu?`

Examples:

- Idu enu?
- Adu enu?

### ___ eshtu?

Meaning: How much/how many ___?

Examples:

- Rate eshtu?
- Bill eshtu?
- Idannu eshtu?

### ___ ideya?

Meaning: Is ___ there/available?

Examples:

- Coffee ideya?
- UPI ideya?
- Change ideya?

### Yaava ___?

Meaning: Which ___?

Examples:

- Yaava route?
- Yaava gate?
- Yaava room?

### ___ elli?

Meaning: Where is ___?

Pattern: `Place/Object + elli?`

Examples:

- Lift elli?
- Office elli?
- Washroom elli?

### ___ yaaru?

Meaning: Who is ___?

Pattern: `Person + yaaru?`

Examples:

- Avru yaaru?
- Neevu yaaru?
- Owner yaaru?

## Need And Want

### ___ beku

Meaning: I want/need ___

Examples:

- Water beku.
- Tea beku.
- Bill beku.

### ___ beda

Meaning: I do not want ___

Examples:

- Coffee beda.
- Tea beda.
- Bill beda.

### Innu ___ beku

Meaning: I want more ___

Examples:

- Innu water beku.
- Innu coffee beku.
- Innu packet beku.

### Saaku

Meaning: Enough / that is enough.

Examples:

- Saaku.
- Ella saaku.

## Location

### Idu ___ / Adu ___

Meaning: This is ___ / That is ___

Examples:

- Idu office.
- Adu mane.

### Idu ___ alla

Meaning: This is not ___

Examples:

- Idu shop alla.
- Idu water alla.

### ___ alli

Meaning: in/at ___ or there, depending on context.

Examples:

- Bengaluru alli.
- Office alli.
- Apartment alli.

### ___ ide / ___ illa

Meaning: ___ is there / ___ is not there.

Examples:

- Water ide.
- Coffee illa.
- Stock illa.

### ___ olage / ___ horage

Meaning: inside ___ / outside ___

Examples:

- Room olage.
- Gate horage.

## Movement

### Place + action

Examples:

- Illi banni. = Come here.
- Alli hogi. = Go there.
- Illi nilli. = Stop/stand here.
- Illi koothkolli. = Sit here.

### Direction + hogi

Examples:

- Left hogi.
- Right hogi.
- Straight hogi.

### Action + madi

Examples:

- Stop madi.
- Left turn madi.
- Right turn madi.

### ___ kodi / ___ tagoli

Examples:

- Bill kodi. = Give the bill.
- Idu tagoli. = Take this.

### ___ heli / ___ torisi

Examples:

- Address heli. = Tell the address.
- Room torisi. = Show the room.

### Idannu ___ / Adannu ___

Meaning: Do an action to this/that object.

Examples:

- Idannu kodi. = Give this.
- Adannu kodi. = Give that.
- Idannu nodi. = Look at this.

### Quantity + item + action

Examples:

- Ondu tea kodi. = Give one tea.
- Eradu coffee kodi. = Give two coffees.
- Ondu packet togothini. = I will take one packet.

### ___ kalisi

Meaning: Send ___

Examples:

- Location kalisi.
- Number kalisi.

## Time And Plans

### Neevu ___?

Meaning: Will you ___?

Examples:

- Neevu barthira?
- Neevu hogthira?
- Neevu madthira?
- Ivaga barthira?

### Naan ___

Meaning: I am/will ___

Examples:

- Naan barthini.
- Naan hogthini.
- Naan madthini.
- Ivaga barthini.

### ___ madthini

Meaning: I will do ___

Examples:

- Payment madthini.
- Scan madthini.

### ___ togothini

Meaning: I will take ___

Examples:

- Idannu togothini.
- Adannu togothini.
- Ondu packet togothini.

### ___ hogbeku / ___ barbeku

Meaning: Need to go to ___ / need to come to ___

Examples:

- Office hogbeku.
- Metro hogbeku.
- Gate horage barbeku.

### ___ aadmele ___ madi

Meaning: After ___, do ___

Examples:

- Reach aadmele call madi.
- Pickup aadmele call madi.

## Lessons 025-072 Pattern Bank

### Identity And Social

- Nanna ___ ___ = My ___ is ___
- Nimma ___ enu? = What is your ___?
- Neevu ellinda? = Where are you from?
- Naan ___ inda = I am from ___
- Kannada swalpa barutte = I know a little Kannada

### Time And Routine

- Time + sentence: Ivattu office hogbeku
- Yaavaga ___? = When ___?
- Yaava time ___? = What time ___?
- Daily/Sometimes/Usually + action

### Problems

- ___ agalla = ___ does not work/happen
- ___ illa = no ___
- Object + check/fix/repair madi
- Person + kalisi = send person
- ___ tagondu banni = bring ___

### Past

- Naan ___ hogidde = I went to ___
- Object + kondide = bought object
- Naan call/message madide = I called/messaged
- En aaytu? = What happened?
- Reason + adakke + result

### Opinions

- Nanage ___ ishta = I like ___
- Nanage ___ ishta illa = I do not like ___
- ___ chennagide = ___ is good
- Artha aaytu/agilla = understood/did not understand
- Nimge yaavdu ishta? = Which one do you like?

### Natural Speech

- Innondu sala heli = Say one more time
- Slow aagi heli = Say slowly
- Beda bidi = Leave it/no need
- Aaytu = Done/okay
- Nodona = Let us see
